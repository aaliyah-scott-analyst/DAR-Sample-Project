#### Summary
In this project, I will conduct general exploratory analysis on sample university alumni gift data to reveal any trends in the gifts received and the types of donors who have given them. I will also use recency, frequency, and monetary (RFM) analysis to determine which donors my hypothetical Development and Alumni Relations department should prioritize with their outreach.

#### Data
The dataset used for this project was generated by AI. I asked ChatGPT to provide code in Postgresql that would generate a relational database that fits the context of the Business Intelligence Analyst Associate position. To do this, I used keywords from the job listing as well as general information about Northwestern. 

Here is part of my prompt: “The role is within the Alumni Relations & Development department of a T20 research university. The job summary specifically mentions that I'll be working with "fundraising and advancement" data.”

For more information on the relational database I used, please see the “AI Sample Database Tables - Snapshot” document.
